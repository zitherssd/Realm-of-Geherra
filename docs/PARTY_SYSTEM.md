# PARTY_SYSTEM.md

This document describes **Party behavior** on the world map.

Parties are data objects; systems interpret them for movement, encounters, and interactions.

---

## 1. Responsibilities

* Hold units and commanders
* Track world position and speed
* Carry shared inventory and gold
* Participate in encounters and interactions

---

## 2. World Map Behavior

* Parties move in continuous space
* The player party advances world time when moving
* AI parties do not advance time

---

## 3. Inventory Behavior

* Inventory is shared across commanders
* Equipping removes item from inventory
* Unequipping returns item to inventory

---

## 4. Encounter Behavior

* Encounters are triggered by proximity
* Interactions are generated by the interaction system
